El Pollo Loco

Game Information

Game Rules

  • Collect coins to increase your score.
  • Pick up bottles to use as weapons.
  • Avoid enemies or lose health.
  • Throw bottles at the final boss to defeat it.
  • Defeat the boss to win the game!
  • Reach the end of the level to complete it.

Controls

  • Spacebar: Jump
  • ⬅: Move left
  • ➡: Move right
  • Bottles: Throw bottles
